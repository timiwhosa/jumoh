Course Description

Graphics Design is a course that explores graphic communication through the understanding of elements and principles of design; as well as, the design process, from idea development through the final execution of a document.

This course introduces students to the characteristics manner of expressions, basic designs, various constructions, and execution of graphics design, and its visual aesthetics at particular times and places throughout history. The course covers; Fundamentals of Graphics Designs, Adobe Illustrator usage, and Adobe photoshop usage.

At the end of this course, you must be proficient enough to go through designs from scratch, and efficiently make use of the tools embedded in the Adobe Suite (Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop), with a good application of elements and principles of designs.

Duration 2 months

Lessons 3 times weekly
