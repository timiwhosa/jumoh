About Jumoh

Jumoh is a real live learning platform where tech skills like Programming, Design, Animation are taught with real-life practical exposure.

Our Mission

We are Transforming the world of physical and online learning with live-based learning and virtual Technology. We are working with tutors who have been in their Subject field for more than 4 years with adequate real exposure. with our platform, we are working on making everybody learners learn at their own pace.

Our Students

Jumoh students will be global and community experts solving problems in their various expertise. we are working on engaging and connecting our students with top industries after graduation.

Our Story

Learners from developing countries like Nigeria are finding it difficult to relate and cope with recorded-based video learning platforms, Jumoh was founded to pursue a mission of bridging the gap between physical and online learning with live-based learning.
